Chris Dean Davis is a portrait photographer based in Hamilton, New Zealand, with over 10 years of experience capturing people through both studio and on-location work. Known for his refined use of light and composition, Chris creates images that are at once bold and quietly intimate.

His work blends minimalism with emotional depth — often balancing controlled setups with natural gestures to produce portraits that feel timeless, cinematic, and human. Whether shooting in-studio or outdoors, Chris brings a calm and intentional presence to every session, drawing out genuine connection through simplicity and light.

Available for portrait sessions, collaborative projects, and editorial work.
